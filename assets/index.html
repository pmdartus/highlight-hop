Walkthrough

Step 1: Open the book, tap the center of the screen and then on the Menu icon

Step 2: Navigate to Annotations

Step 3: Tap on the Share icon

Step 4: Tap on Export

Step 5: Enter one of the email addresses listed above and press Send.
